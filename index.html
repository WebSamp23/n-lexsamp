ABOUT US

What Is N-LEX?

N-LEX

N-LEX(Nail Lovers Express Service) is a service that offers customization and personalization and nail enhancement for men and women including manicure, pedicure and nail enhancement such as acrylic, nail extension and gel nails.

The goal of N-LEX is to provide a relaxing and enjoyable experience for customers while delivering high quality nail services. In connection with the tagline it believes "nails that stun, service that's fun!".

Nail Art

Nail art, the practice of painting decorative designs on your fingernails, is a fun way to brighten up your everyday look or accessorize a special occasion outfit. There are a variety of designs available, ranging from subtle and understated to funky and outrageous.

Nail Extension

Simply put, gel nail extensions consist of hard gel polish that’s cured with a UV light over the top of your natural nail. To begin with, a nail artist or nail technician would attach a nail form underneath the tip of the nail, lengthening it. The hard gel goes on top and is sculpted to perfection. That’s the classic gel manicure technique, but it’s not the only application process.
